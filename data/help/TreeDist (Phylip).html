Websitehttp://evolution.genetics.washington.edu/phylip/

DescriptionThis program computes distances between trees. Two distances are computed, the Branch Score Distance of Kuhner and Felsenstein (1994), and the more widely known Symmetric Difference of Robinson and Foulds (1981).
